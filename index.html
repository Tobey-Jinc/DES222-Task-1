In the time of distance
Relationships with remote others
In a time where we have access to everyone, and everyone has access to us, we feel the most alone.
How can we maintain and create real and meaningful connections in a digital world?
Two things: touch (haptic response) and...
...holographic face to face video calls.
Utilizing these two technologies will help make online interactions more like in-person interactions.
For the haptic touch, gloves could be used as the easiest and cheapest solution.
But something without gloves would be better. Ultrasonic speakers could be used to create haptic touch without gloves.
People could be scanned as a 3D model and then projected into the recipient's room.
This combination of haptic touch and 3D holographic projection will make online interactions more genuine...
...and close the distance between us.
